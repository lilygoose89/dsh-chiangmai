This workshop is for you if:

  • You have already started your pitch deck, but you don’t know who to send it to.
  • The pitch seems good on paper, but you’re not confident when presenting it.
  • Investors are intrigued by your idea, but don’t seem to *get it* yet.
  • Your company is ready to grow and scale with the right support.

This workshop is not for you if:

  • You’ve just started and you’re not sure yet what funding you need.
  • Your idea or IP cannot be shared openly without an NDA.
  • You just want to make a good presentation for the sake of an award.

THIS WORKSHOP IS DESIGNED TO GET YOU ON THE PATH TO FUNDRAISING

Suitable for all funding stages.

 

 

Agenda

 

Day 1: Perfect Your Slide Deck

  • What makes a good slide deck?
  • What convinces an investor to take a meeting?
  • How do you tell a good story?
  • Which order is the most compelling and what slides do you actually need?
  • How does good design help you make your argument?

Along with guidance from mentors, you will discuss and share ideas with your fellow startups on how to improve and perfect your pitch deck. 

 

Day 2: Nail Your Elevator Pitch

  • How do you explain your startup in less than 3 minutes?
  • What are common questions people have for you and what’s confusing about your pitch?
  • What is essential to your business and what’s fluff?
  • Exactly how much information do you share to start, and what do you save for behind closed doors?

This is the day to practice your presentation skills. Founders will each get a turn to pitch in front of the group to receive coaching and feedback. Elevator pitches will be revised, drilled and perfected so they become second-nature.

 

Beyond the pitch itself, we will also discuss:

  • How do you turn an elevator pitch into an opportunity to secure investment?
  • Where and how do you send your pitch deck to get investment?
  • What else do you need to learn before you’re ready to get investment?
